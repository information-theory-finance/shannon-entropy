======================================================
Shannon Entropy — Information Theory × NSE Finance
======================================================

.. image:: https://img.shields.io/badge/version-0.1.0-amber
   :alt: Version 0.1.0

.. image:: https://img.shields.io/badge/python-3.10%2B-blue
   :alt: Python 3.10+

----

Shannon entropy is the foundational quantity of information theory, measuring
the average uncertainty of a random variable. Applied to NSE financial data,
it quantifies the unpredictability of return distributions, the concentration
of order-book liquidity, and the information content embedded in price
processes — enabling a rigorous, measure-theoretic view of market structure.
